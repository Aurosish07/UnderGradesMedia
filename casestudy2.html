Overview

YELO is a bike service app in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, offering doorstep two-wheeler repair and maintenance. Customers can book services, track repairs, and pay online through the app and website.
From oil changes and brake repairs to full bike inspections, YELO ensures affordable, hassle-free servicing at home with certified mechanics.

By partnering with UnderGrads Media, YELO built a strong brand identity, website, app, and digital marketing strategy, transforming into a trusted on-demand bike service platform for Odisha’s riders.

The Challenge & Solution

Challenges Before Collaboration

  • Limited Digital Presence: The company lacked an official website and mobile app for customer bookings and interactions.
  • Undefined Brand Identity: The company needed a coherent brand strategy, including a logo, color scheme, and consistent messaging.
  • Minimal Marketing Strategy: The company had no established social media presence or targeted marketing campaigns.
  • Inefficient Operations: Manual booking systems caused scheduling conflicts and reduced customer satisfaction.
  • Recruitment Issues: The company lacked standardized procedures for hiring qualified mechanics and support staff.

After Our Role & Execution

  • Website & App Development: Built a responsive website yelo.co.in with service listings, pricing, and booking features & Developed a mobile app for seamless service booking, status tracking, and payments.
  • Branding & Identity: Created the brand name "YELO" and crafted a compelling tagline & Designed a modern logo and established comprehensive brand guidelines with color palettes and typography
  • Digital Marketing & Social Media Management: Built YELO's presence across Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn & Created and managed content calendars, engaging posts, and targeted ad campaigns to grow customer base.
  • Advertising & Promotions: Created promotional materials including banners and flyers & Launched targeted local advertising campaigns to boost brand awareness
  • Photography & Videography: Produced professional photo content featuring services, customer testimonials, and behind-the-scenes operations.
  • Recruitment & HR Support: Created clear hiring procedures and job descriptions for mechanics and support staff & Supported the recruitment process to ensure qualified professional hires.
